# About Me

I’m **Bin**. I’m not an "automotive influencer" or a self-proclaimed expert. I am a regular car owner who enjoys deconstructing the engineering logic behind mechanical systems.

## Why This Site?
The automotive market is saturated with fragmented information, exaggerated marketing claims, and unverified "old wives' tales." Inaccurate maintenance habits don't just waste money—they compromise vehicle safety.

I started these notes as a personal discipline to filter out the noise and keep my own maintenance decisions grounded in facts.

## My Approach
Every entry on this site is developed through a rigorous verification process:
* **Data-Driven**: I prioritize Official Service Manuals and international standards (e.g., API, ACEA, SAE) over marketing brochures.
* **Cross-Referencing**: I cross-check technical forum data with real-world insights from experienced technicians.
* **No Hype**: My goal is to record verified facts and personal observations, stripped of sales talk.

## Reader's Disclaimer
* **Not Professional Advice**: These notes are for informational purposes only. Always consult a certified professional before performing major repairs.
* **Open to Feedback**: If you spot an error in my data, please reach out. I value accuracy over being "right" and will update the content accordingly.
