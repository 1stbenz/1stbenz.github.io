  - question: "What is the standard cleaning order for car detailing DIY?"
    answer: "The standard sequence is to clean the Interior first, followed by the Wheels, and finally the Exterior."
  - question: "Which products are recommended for interior cleaning and maintenance?"
    answer: "For cleaning, Meguiar's D101, SONAX Multi-Purpose Cleaner, or Collinite #855 are recommended. For maintenance and UV protection, 3M PN38143, Meguiar's Ultimate Protectant/Interior Detailer, or Turtle Wax DASH & GLASS/INSIDE & OUT PROTECTANT are great choices."
  - question: "What are the complete steps for exterior detailing?"
    answer: "The full exterior process is: Wash > Clay > Compound > Polish > Glaze > Sealant > Wax > Detailer."
  - question: "What are Compound/Correction products, and what do they do?"
    answer: "These products contain abrasives used to remove clear coat defects such as swirl marks or scratches. Heavy compounds are for aggressive correction, while polishes or cleaner waxes contain mild abrasives for finishing."
  - question: "What is the purpose of a Glaze?"
    answer: "Glazes are typically non-abrasive and offer no long-term protection; they are designed to fill minor swirls and enhance the depth and gloss of the paint."
  - question: "What does a Sealant do?"
    answer: "A Sealant is a synthetic wax designed for durability, providing long-lasting protection against corrosion and environmental contaminants."
  - question: "What are the characteristics of Carnauba Wax?"
    answer: "Carnauba Wax is a natural wax used to enhance the 'wet look' and brilliance of the paint. It is often layered on top of a sealant for maximum aesthetic effect."
  - question: "What is the use of Spray Wax / Detailers?"
    answer: "Quick Detailers are primarily for light cleaning and lubrication. Spray Waxes/Sealants are used during the drying process to remove dust or to top up existing paint protection."

**Standard Detailing Order: Interior > Wheel > Exterior**

### Interior

* **Interior Cleaning**
    * Meguiar's D101 Detailer All Purpose Cleaner (Dilution 1:10)
    * SONAX Multi-Purpose Auto Interior Cleaner
    * Collinite Leather & Vinyl Treatment Wax #855 (Cleans and protects)
* **Interior Care (Protection/UV Resistance/Glow)**
    * 3M PN38143 Leather & Vinyl Restorer
    * Meguiar's Ultimate Protectant, G14716
    * Meguiar's Ultimate Interior Detailer, G16216
    * Turtle Wax DASH & GLASS INTERIOR DETAILER

**Exterior Steps: Wash > Clay > Compound > Polish > Glaze > Sealant > Wax > Detailer**

### Entry Level Product Recommendations (Rookie Detailers)

#### 1. Compound / Correction (Removing Swirls & Scratches)
> *These products contain abrasives to remove clear coat imperfections.*
* **Heavy Cut (Aggressive Correction):**
    * Finish Kare BWM 114: Rubbing Compound
    * Poorboy's Master Cut Super Cut Compound
    * Poorboy's SSR3
    * Meguiar's Ultimate Compound, G17216
* **Polish / AIO (Fine Polish/Cleaner Wax - Mild Abrasives):**
    * Turtle Wax Hybrid Solutions Ceramic Polish & Wax (AIO)
    * Turtle Wax Hybrid Solutions Pro 1 & Done (Adjustable abrasive)
    * Collinite 631 (Fleetwax) / 920 (Fiberglass Cleaner)

#### 2. Glaze (Filling Defects & Enhancing Gloss)
> *Typically non-abrasive and non-protective; used specifically to "fill" swirls for depth.*
* Poorboy's White Diamond (For light colors) / Black Hole (For dark colors)
* 3M Hand Glaze 5990 (Pure filler, no protection)

#### 3. Sealant (Synthetic Wax - Long-lasting Protection)
> *Synthetic products focused on durability and resistance.*
* **Finish Kare 1000P**: Hi-Temp Paste Wax (Legendary durability)
* Finish Kare 2180 / 118 (Sealant Conditioner)
* Meguiar's M21 Synthetic Sealant
* Poorboy's EX Sealant with Carnauba
* 3M 39030 Performance Finish
* Collinite 476S / 885 (Durable detergent-proof wax)
* Meguiar's M27 SiO2 (Plastic Safe)
* Turtle Wax Ice Paste Wax
* Turtle Wax Hybrid Solutions Pro to The Max Wax (Liquid Sealant)

#### 4. Carnauba Wax (Natural Wax - Enhancing the "Wet Look")
> *Focuses on brilliance and water beading; best layered over a sealant.*
* Finish Kare #2685: Cherry Pink Paste Wax
* 3M Perfect-It Boat Wax 36112/36113
* Collinite 915 (Marque D'Elegance) / 845 (Insulator Wax)
* Poorboy's World Liquid Natty's Blue / Red
* Turtle Wax Super Hard Shell (Classic hard shell protection)

#### 5. Spray Wax / Detailer (Drying Aid/Dusting/Protection Top-up)
* **Quick Detailer (Cleaning/Lubrication):**
    * Meguiar's Quik Detailer Mist & Wipe, A3316
    * Meguiar's D155 Last Touch Spray Detailer
* **Spray Wax/Sealant (Maintenance & Protection):**
    * Meguiar's Ultimate Quik Detailer, G14422 (UQD)
    * Meguiar's M799 Pro Hybrid Ceramic Bead Booster
    * Turtle Wax Hybrid Solutions Ceramic Wax 3-in-1 Detailer
    * Turtle Wax Express Shine Carnauba Spray
    * Turtle Wax Pro Graphene Flex Wax
    * Turtle Wax Ceramic Spray Wax Coating (CSC)
