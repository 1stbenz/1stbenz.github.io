## Signs of Battery Aging

- **Idle Stop-Start (Eco Mode) Disappears:** The most obvious sign is the "A" icon on the dashboard remaining yellow or the engine no longer shutting off at red lights. This happens because the BMS detects the battery's health or charge level is below the required threshold.
- **Maintenance Alerts:** Technicians will check the battery health during regular service. If they mention the battery is deformed or showing low voltage, it's time to plan a replacement.
- **Sluggish Starts:** If the ignition doesn't feel as "sharp" as it used to, don't wait—detecting or replacing the battery immediately can prevent you from being stranded.

## Why I Chose Green Run 2 (Great Power)

- **Excluding Small Manufacturers:** I avoid brands with unknown factory addresses or those that primarily use "marketing hype" rather than technical data.
- **Safety First:** Great Power uses cells with legitimate automotive-grade non-combustible certifications. For a component sitting in the engine bay, this is non-negotiable.
- **Value for Money:** It provides the best balance between performance, warranty, and price, especially for W205 owners who want to eliminate AGM issues without spending a fortune.

### Advantages of LiFePO4 vs. AGM:
1.  **Weight:** Reduced by nearly **20kg**, significantly improving front-end handling.
2.  **Voltage Stability:** Higher and more stable voltage results in better ignition and smoother transmission shifts.
3.  **Longevity:** Can last 5-10 years, outperforming typical AGM batteries which often fail after 2-3 years in high-heat environments.

### **Installation Note**
Always use an **OBD-II Memory Saver** during the swap. The W205 is sensitive to power loss; maintaining backup power ensures you won't have to deal with reset clocks, lost seat memories, or ECU error codes.
