### Recommended Concentration
* Diesel < 1%
* Paraffin < 0.2%
* Cleaning Naphtha < 0.05%

### Pre-Mix Ratio
> **D : P : C = 2L : 0.5L : 0.1L**

### Recommended Additive Ratio
